nibbles by jbytheway

Single- and multi-player snake game

Nibbles is a snake game inspired by the QBASIC game of the same name, but implemented in C++. It uses a client/server architecture with communications over TCP. It strives to simulate the gameplay experience of the original and does not provide gratuitous graphical advances, although the details of the levels are different.
